Calendar Math for 10 Days

Calculating 10 days forward requires knowing month lengths. January, March, May, July, August, October, December have 31 days. April, June, September, November have 30 days. February has 28 (or 29 in leap years). Starting January 15, adding 10 days: 10 days = January reaches day 25.

The day of the week follows a predictable pattern: 10 days = 1 complete weeks plus 3 extra days. So 10 days from Monday lands on Friday. This works regardless of the starting date's specific date number.
